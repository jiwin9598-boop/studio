# **App Name**: StudyBuddy AI

## Core Features:

- AI Tutoring Tool: Provides personalized study assistance, answering questions and explaining concepts with reasoning. Functions as a personalized tool, helping students understand complex subjects effectively.
- Adaptive Quizzes: Generates quizzes tailored to the student's learning level and progress, adapting difficulty based on performance.
- Study Plan Generator: Automatically create a structured study plan based on exam dates, topics, and the student's available time, including prioritized topics based on difficulty.
- Progress Tracking: Visualizes student progress, showing areas of strength and those needing improvement, to help students focus their efforts.
- Resource Library: Stores and categorizes study materials, including notes, articles, and videos, making resources easily accessible to students.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: Soft blue (#64B5F6) to promote a calm and focused learning environment.
- Background color: Very light blue (#E3F2FD) for a gentle, unobtrusive backdrop.
- Accent color: Muted orange (#FFB74D) to highlight important interface elements and encourage interaction.
- Body and headline font: 'PT Sans', a humanist sans-serif combining modernity and warmth for easy reading.
- Use simple, clear icons to represent different subjects, study tools, and progress indicators.
- Incorporate subtle, non-distracting animations for feedback, such as smooth transitions when switching between study modules or confirming actions.
